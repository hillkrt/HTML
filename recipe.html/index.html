FLOUR COOKIES

Ingredients

    1 cup butter (2 sticks or 250 grams), at room temperature
    ¼ cup Sunflower Oil
    5 cups flour
    1 cup corn starch
    1 cup powdered sugar
    1 pkg (1 1/2 teaspoons or 5 grams) Vanilla Powder
    Powdered sugar, to garnish
    Cacao power – optional to taste

Directions

    In a medium bowl, combine butter and powdered sugar.
    Add Sunflower oil
    Add flour, vanilla powder and corn starch


    Mixing wet and dry ingredients in a medium bowl.

    After flour is gradually added and combined, knead dough until it is
    soft and non sticky dough. You might need to add a little more flour or reduce it a little depending on the dough.

    After dough is kneaded, allow dough to rest in refrigerator for 5 minutes.


    Separate a portion of the dough and roll in cacao (cocoa powder).
    is optional but it will add a hint of chocolate flavor to the cookies.


    A portion of the dough was separated into 2 halves.
    This half was rolled and mixed with cacao powder and rolled into tiny balls.

    Allow dough to rest in refrigerator for 5 minutes.
    Meanwhile, preheat oven to 325 degrees F (160 degrees C).
    Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
    If you like cacao, separate dough and add cocoa powder to dough


    Dough was rolled into small balls between the hands.
    The chocolate flour balls were pressed into the flour cookie balls.

    Roll small balls between hands and place on baking tray. If using the cacao (coco powder) balls, press into flour cookie balls and seen above in the image.
    Bake for 20-25 minutes.
    Remove from oven and allow to rest for 5 minutes. Transfer to a wire rack and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
    Bon appetit
